All of the building regulations and technical guidance documents, right in your pocket.

I'll get back to you on that...

Nobody knows ALL of the building regulations and technical guidance documents off by heart. Well maybe some do, but they need to get out more! Sometimes, situations come up where you can't quite remember the specifics of a particular requirement. If one of these situations happens to be a question directed towards you during a meeting, the response is usually "I'll check that out in the office and get back to you".

Most of the time this is fine, you can simply look up the relevant material when you're back at the office and send off an email with your response. However, wouldn't it be more efficient to be able to respond there and then, putting the issue to bed and removing one of your required actions from the meeting's minutes.

You could always carry around hardcopies of all the documents you might possibly need. Although you may raise a few eyebrows when you walk into a meeting pushing a hand truck laden with boxes. Better yet, if the documents are available online you could try to find the right website and sieve through the documents until you find the one you're after.

Surely there's a better way?

Enter, Pocket TGD

I decided to develop Pocket TGD to help myself and my colleagues. The aim was to provide quick, easy access to reference material, no matter where the user is. I explain and demonstrate the concept within the video provided. (Pocket TGD was my first app, I was pretty happy to see it on the App Store!)

Pocket TGD is aimed particularly at engineers, architects, project managers and other professionals involved in the design and construction of schools in Ireland. It brings together content from a number of government bodies into one central, simple index.

The app was an instant hit. We were able to resolve issues much more quickly on site and in meetings. No more having to check things later. Naturally, other design team members and contractors started to use the app too. It is free to download after all.
